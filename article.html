Tableware from La Tour d'Argent sold at auction in Paris

By Sophie Bouchet

january 14,2024

Until January 19, 2024, Artcurial is organizing a digital auction during which tableware from the famous Parisian restaurant La Tour d'Argent is sold.

just reopened after a metamorphosis orchestrated by architect Franklin Azzi , La Tour d'Argent is organizing an auction in collaboration with Artcurial. This event takes place only online until January 19, 2024. It brings together 320 lots collected over several centuries by the Parisian institution. The restaurant, whose origins date back to 1582, essentially sheds pieces of tableware such as porcelain plates, crystal glasses, etc. Furniture is also sold, including a chandelier, a mirror, seats, etc. Lots which start at a price of 60 euros and allow you to own a small part of this emblematic address of French gastronomy.
